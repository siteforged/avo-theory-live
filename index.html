Our Story

We're obsessed with the perfect avocado

Avo Theory began as a tiny pop-up stall at the Austin Downtown Farmers' Market, where co-founders Maya Rivera and Jake Okonkwo hand-mashed guac for curious crowds every Saturday morning. The response was overwhelming.

Today we operate a full-service kitchen rooted in the same philosophy: exceptional produce, honest preparation, zero compromise. Every avocado that enters our kitchen is inspected, every dish is made to order, and every guest is treated like a friend.
